Menu

Pho

The secret behind our authentic Pho is the broth. We spend hours of careful preparation in order to bring out the deep, rich flavor from the beef bones. We use only the freshest meats and vegetables to personally top each of the Phở bowls we serve. Our soup comes with a side of bean sprouts, basil, cilantro, lime wedges, and hot peppers.

Bò 7 Món

Bò 7 Món is a traditional Vietnamese tasting menu of seven beef dishes. The dishes include: Gỏi bò, Bò chả đùm, Bò nhúng dấm, Bò nướng mỡ chài, Bò lá lốt, Bò nướng hành, and Cháo Bò.

Come try it here at Ánh Hồng for an authentic Vietnamese experience!
